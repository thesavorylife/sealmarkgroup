Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2026  ·  Last Updated: July 7, 2026

Sealmark Group Inc, a New York corporation ("Sealmark Group," "we," "our," or "us"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Identification information required for notarization (e.g., government-issued ID details, document type)
  • Document contents shared in connection with notarization, apostille, or loan signing services
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe, see Section 4)
  • Any other information you voluntarily submit through forms, email, or phone

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on the Site, and referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies for analytics and Site functionality

c. Information from Third Parties

  • Identity verification data from RON platforms (e.g., credential analysis, knowledge-based authentication results)
  • Booking information from scheduling tools such as Calendly

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, schedule, and complete the Services you request
  • To verify identity and comply with notarial recordkeeping requirements
  • To process payments and issue receipts
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, and Service-related communications
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
  • To improve the Site and our Services

3. Notarial Records

Notary publics in New York are required to maintain a journal of notarial acts. This journal contains limited information including the date, type of act, signer's name, and identification used. Notarial records are maintained securely and are accessible only as required by law or authorized administrative review.

Remote Online Notarization sessions are recorded and retained for the period required by New York State law. Recordings are stored securely and are not shared except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

4. Payment Processing & Stripe

All payments are processed through Stripe, Inc. ("Stripe"). When you submit payment information, that information is transmitted directly to Stripe over an encrypted connection. Sealmark Group does not store full credit card numbers, CVV codes, or bank account credentials.

Stripe's collection, use, and disclosure of your information is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy, available at stripe.com/privacy.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only as follows:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors (e.g., Stripe, Calendly, RON platforms, email and hosting providers) who assist in operating the Site and providing the Services, under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, subpoena, or regulatory authority, or to protect our rights and the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a sale, merger, or restructuring of Sealmark Group Inc, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Cookies & Tracking

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies to enable Site functionality, remember preferences, and analyze traffic. You may set your browser to refuse cookies, but some features of the Site may not function properly as a result.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide Services, comply with legal and regulatory obligations (including notarial recordkeeping requirements under New York law), resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@sealmarkgroup.com. Note that some information, including notarial journal entries and RON session recordings, must be retained as required by law and cannot be deleted on request.

10. Children's Privacy

The Site and Services are not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we may delete it.

11. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., Stripe, Calendly). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the Site or Services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
