About Sealmark Group

The story behind the seal.

Built on the conviction that notarization is a quiet act of trust, and that trust deserves attention to the smallest details.

Sealmark Group exists for the moments most people only encounter a few times in a lifetime. The closing on a first home. A power of attorney signed beside a parent's hospital bed. A child's first passport application. The transfer of a family business. An apostille that lets a marriage be recognized in another country.

These moments aren't paperwork. They are milestones. They deserve a notary who treats them that way.

"A notary's seal is small. The trust it represents is not."

Our standards

Every signing follows a deliberate protocol. Identity is verified carefully. Documents are reviewed before the seal touches paper. Journals are kept meticulously. We arrive on time, dress for the occasion, and leave you with copies and confidence.

We are particularly experienced with vulnerable signers such as elderly clients, hospital patients, and individuals with mobility limitations, where attention, patience, and a calm presence matter as much as the seal itself.

Service area & availability

Sealmark Group serves Queens, Brooklyn, Nassau, and Suffolk Counties for in-person and mobile work. Remote Online Notarization is available to signers anywhere in the United States. Same-day appointments are often possible. Call or book online to check availability.
